about ACP

academic focus

The Association for Constraint Programming (ACP) is dedicated to advancing the field of constraint programming, fostering research, education, and collaboration in constraint-based modeling, solving, and applications.

global community

ACP connects researchers, educators, and practitioners worldwide, organizing events, summer schools, and conferences to promote knowledge exchange and innovation in constraint programming.

about jilin university

prestigious history

Founded in 1946, Jilin University (JLU) is one of China's key comprehensive universities under the direct administration of the Ministry of Education. It has developed a multidisciplinary system covering 13 major fields including philosophy, economics, law, science, engineering, medicine, and management.

academic excellence

Jilin University boasts a team of distinguished faculty members, including academicians of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and Engineering. It has state-of-the-art research facilities and fosters a vibrant academic community that promotes innovation and collaboration.

international cooperation

JLU has established cooperative relationships with over 300 universities and research institutions worldwide. It promotes student exchange programs, joint research projects, and academic seminars, fostering a global perspective among students and faculty.
